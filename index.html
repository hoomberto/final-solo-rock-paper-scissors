Battle of the Zodiac

Choose your fighter

Select a sign and try to conquer the zodiac! Using two moves that are randomly set to all signs, a battle between the player and computer begins. Each zodiac sign has an associated element (fire, earth, water, or air) and quality (cardinal, muted, or fixed). The matchup will determine the advantages at play for a given game. The matchups are as follows:

Element Matchups

  • Air --> Earth
  • Air --- Air
  • Air --- Water
  • Air <-- Fire
  • Earth --> Water
  • Earth --- Earth
  • Earth --- Fire
  • Earth <-- Air
  • Water --> Fire
  • Water --- Water
  • Water --- Air
  • Water <--- Earth
  • Fire --> Air
  • Fire --- Fire
  • Fire --- Earth
  • Fire <-- Water

Quality Matchups

  • Cardinal --> Fixed
  • Cardinal --- Cardinal
  • Cardinal <-- Mutable
  • Fixed --> Mutable
  • Fixed --- Fixed
  • Fixed <-- Cardinal
  • Mutable --> Cardinal
  • Mutable --- Mutable
  • Mutable <-- Fixed
